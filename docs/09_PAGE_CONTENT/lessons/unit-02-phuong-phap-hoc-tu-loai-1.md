# Unit 2 — Phương pháp học từ loại (1)

> Học theo "họ từ" (word family): từ một gốc, mở rộng sang các từ loại khác nhau (danh từ, động từ, tính từ, trạng từ).

## 1. responsible

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| responsible (Adj) +for sth / +to sb/sth | chịu trách nhiệm | A drunk man cannot be held fully responsible for his actions. |
| irresponsible (Adj) | vô trách nhiệm | Tom is an irresponsible person. |
| responsibility (N) | sự trách nhiệm | Women bear children and take responsibility for childcare. |
| irresponsibility (N) | sự vô trách nhiệm | They thought that the explosion was an act of irresponsibility. |

## 2. special

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| special (Adj) | đặc biệt | She's a very special person. |
| specially (Adv) | đặc biệt | She has a wheelchair that was specially made for her. |
| speciality (N) | nét đặc trưng, chuyên môn | Unkind remarks are one of his specialities. |
| specialist (N-p) | chuyên gia | My doctor has referred me to a specialist for a second opinion. |
| specialize (V) + in | chuyên về | That restaurant specializes in seafood. |
| specialization (N) | sự chuyên môn hóa | My main specialization was literature. |

## 3. eat

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| eat (V) | ăn | Jane never eats breakfast. |
| edible (Adj/N) | có thể ăn được (không độc) / thức ăn | Only the leaves of the plant are edible. |
| eatable (Adj) | ăn được | Food that is eatable is good enough to eat, but not excellent. |
| eater (N-p) | người ăn | I'm still a big eater. |

## 4. person

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| person (N) — số nhiều: people | con người | Who was the first person to swim the English Channel? |
| personal (Adj) | cá nhân, riêng tư | The book gives little information about his personal life. |
| personally (Adv) | đích thân, theo cá nhân | Personally, I think the show is going to be a great success. |
| personalize (V) | cá nhân hóa | I don't want to personalize the issue. |

## 5. approve

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| approve (V) + of | tán thành, chấp thuận | He doesn't approve of smoking. |
| approval (N) | sự tán thành, sự chấp thuận | He showed his approval by smiling broadly. |
| disapprove (V) + of | phản đối | I strongly disapprove of underage drinking. |
| disapproval (N) | sự không tán thành, sự phản đối | She could sense their disapproval of her suggestion. |

## 6. compare

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| compare (V) + with/to | so sánh | This road is quite busy compared to ours. |
| comparison (N) | sự so sánh | They made a comparison of different countries' eating habits. |
| comparable (Adj) | có thể so sánh | The two experiences aren't comparable. |
| comparative (Adj) | so sánh, tương đối | "Fatter" is the comparative of "fat". |
| comparatively (Adv) | tương đối | Inflation was comparatively low. |
| in comparison with | so sánh với | In comparison with the French, the British eat far less fish. |

## 7. frequent

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| frequent (Adj) | thường xuyên | The most frequent cause of death is heart disease. |
| frequency (N) | tính thường xuyên | Complaints about the frequency of buses rose in the last year. |
| infrequent (Adj) | không đều đặn | His letters became infrequent, then stopped completely. |
| infrequency (N) | sự không thường xuyên | She did notice and comment on the infrequency of our visits. |

## 8. history

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| history (N) | lịch sử | I studied modern European history at college. |
| historic (Adj) | có tính chất lịch sử | How many historic buildings are damaged by fire each year? |
| historical (Adj) | thuộc lịch sử | She specializes in historical novels set in 18th-century England. |
| historically (Adv) | về mặt lịch sử | The film makes no attempt to be historically accurate. |

## 9. confident

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| confident (Adj) | tự tin, tin cậy, chắc chắn | I'm confident of his skills as a manager. |
| confidence (N) | sự tự tin, sự tin cậy | She's completely lacking in confidence. |
| confidential (Adj) | bí mật, riêng tư | A person's medical records are confidential. |
| confidentiality (N) | sự cẩn mật, sự tin cẩn | All replies will be treated with complete confidentiality. |
| confide in sb | giãi bày tâm sự | As sisters, they have always confided in each other. |
| self-confident (Adj) | tự tin về bản thân | He is self-confident anywhere he goes. |

## 10. recognize

| Word | Nghĩa | Ví dụ |
|------|-------|-------|
| recognize (V) | nhận ra, thừa nhận | I hadn't seen her for 20 years, but I recognized her immediately. |
| recognizable (Adj) | có thể nhận ra | The Eiffel Tower is an instantly recognizable landmark. |
| recognition (N) | sự nhận ra, sự thừa nhận | His work was slow to gain recognition. |
| unrecognizable (Adj) | không thể nhận ra | His voice was muffled by his collar and unrecognizable. |

## PRACTICE

1. The judge told him it was _______ to drink and drive and banned him for a year.
   A. responsible  B. irresponsible  C. responsibility  D. irresponsibility
2. Most doctors are _________ after they learn general medicine.
   A. special  B. specialists  C. especially  D. specialist
3. Peter's gained so much weight because he can't stop ______.
   A. eat  B. edible  C. eatable  D. overeating
4. The sun and the moon are often ________ in poetry.
   A. person  B. personal  C. personalize  D. personified
5. She wants to be an actress but her parents ________ of her intention.
   A. approve of  B. approval  C. disapprove  D. disapproval
6. In the Middle Ages, salt was very valuable so that slaves were _______ with it.
   A. compare  B. comparable  C. comparative  D. incomparable
7. The ________ of the trains and the buses causes frustration and annoyance.
   A. frequent  B. frequency  C. infrequent  D. infrequency
8. It is usually forbidden to destroy _______ buildings.
   A. history  B. historic  C. historical  D. historically
9. This matter is very _______. Don't discuss it outside the office.
   A. confident  B. confidential  C. confidence  D. confide
10. I didn't know who she was with a mask on. She was completely ________.
    A. recognize  B. recognizable  C. recognition  D. unrecognizable

## Đáp án & giải thích

> Claude tự giải (file gốc không có đáp án) — bạn nên rà lại.

1. **B. irresponsible** — sau "was" cần **tính từ**; nghĩa tiêu cực (vô trách nhiệm) → bị cấm lái xe.
2. **B. specialists** — "doctors are ___" cần **danh từ số nhiều**; bác sĩ trở thành chuyên gia.
3. **D. overeating** — sau "stop" dùng **V-ing**; overeating (ăn quá nhiều) → tăng cân.
4. **D. personified** — mặt trời/mặt trăng được **nhân hóa** (personify) trong thơ.
5. **C. disapprove** — có "but" → cha mẹ **phản đối**; cần động từ "disapprove of".
6. **B. comparable** — "be + adj + with"; muối quý đến mức nô lệ có giá trị **so sánh được** với nó.
7. **D. infrequency** — service thưa thớt **gây bực bội** → danh từ nghĩa tiêu cực "infrequency".
8. **B. historic** — trước danh từ "buildings" cần **tính từ**; historic = có ý nghĩa lịch sử.
9. **B. confidential** — sau "be" cần **tính từ**; confidential = bí mật (đừng bàn ngoài văn phòng).
10. **D. unrecognizable** — đeo mặt nạ nên **không thể nhận ra** → tính từ unrecognizable.

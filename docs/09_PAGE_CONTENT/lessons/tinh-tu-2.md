# Tính từ (2) — Tính từ đi với giới từ

## VI. Tính từ đi với giới từ

### A
- **afraid of** — sợ hãi. *Ex:* Are you afraid of snakes?
- **angry / annoyed / furious about sth / with sb for doing sth** — tức giận. *Ex:* I was angry with myself for making such a stupid mistake.
- **accustomed to V-ing** (= used to V-ing) — quen với. *Ex:* I am accustomed to driving on the left.
- **addicted to sth** — nghiện. *Ex:* He's addicted to computer games.
- **anxious about sth / for sb** — lo lắng. *Ex:* He seemed anxious about the meeting.
- **ashamed of** — hổ thẹn. *Ex:* She was deeply ashamed of her behaviour.
- **amazed / astonished at / by sth/sb** — ngạc nhiên. *Ex:* We were amazed by his generosity.
- **acquainted with** — quen với (hay dùng: get acquainted with). *Ex:* The students are acquainted with the work of Shakespeare.
- **allergic to** — bị dị ứng. *Ex:* I'm allergic to cats.
- **appreciated for** — được đánh giá cao. *Ex:* I was appreciated for my help.
- **associated with** — liên quan/cộng tác với. *Ex:* He no longer wished to be associated with the party's policy.
- **aware of** (>< unaware of) — nhận thức về. *Ex:* He was well aware of the problem.
- **attached to** — gắn bó với. *Ex:* We've grown very attached to this house.

### B
- **bad at** — không giỏi. *Ex:* I'm bad at Maths.
- **bored with** — chán. *Ex:* I'm bored with my job.
- **brilliant at** — rất giỏi. *Ex:* He's brilliant at football.
- **based on** — dựa trên. *Ex:* This novel is based on a real story.
- **beneficial to** — có lợi cho. *Ex:* A good diet is beneficial to health.
- **boastful for / about** — ba hoa, khoác lác. *Ex:* I don't want to sound boastful.
- **busy with sth/sb** (busy doing sth) — bận. *Ex:* They were busy with preparations for their wedding.

### C
- **clever at** — thông minh. *Ex:* She's clever at getting what she wants.
- **crazy about** — điên cuồng vì. *Ex:* He's crazy about you.
- **capable of** — có khả năng. *Ex:* He is capable of anything.
- **committed to** (= devoted/dedicated to) — tận tâm. *Ex:* She is committed to her family.
- **concerned about / for sth** — lo lắng. *Ex:* The President is concerned about this issue.
- **content with** — bằng lòng. *Ex:* She seemed content with her results.
- **careful with / about / of sth** — cẩn thận. *Ex:* Please be careful with my glasses.
- **certain about / of** — chắc chắn. *Ex:* You don't seem certain about it.
- **characteristic of** — đặc trưng cho. *Ex:* Enthusiasm is characteristic of him.
- **connected with** — liên hệ với. *Ex:* The fall in sales is connected with the decline in employment.
- **conscious of** — nhận thức, chú ý. *Ex:* She's very conscious of the problems.
- **crowded with** (= packed with) — chật kín. *Ex:* a room crowded with books.
- **curious about** — tò mò. *Ex:* Children are curious about animals.

### D
- **disappointed at / by / with / in** — thất vọng. *Ex:* I was disappointed by the quality of the wine.
- **derived from** — bắt nguồn từ. *Ex:* "fiction" is derived from the Greek "fictus".
- **dedicated / devoted to** — tận tụy. *Ex:* She is dedicated to her job.
- **discouraged by** — bị nhụt chí. *Ex:* He sounded discouraged by the lack of progress.
- **dissatisfied with** — không hài lòng. *Ex:* He's dissatisfied with his life.
- **doubtful about** — hoài nghi. *Ex:* Rose was doubtful about the whole idea.
- **delighted at / by / with** — vui mừng. *Ex:* She was delighted at the news.

### E
- **excited about** — có hứng thú. *Ex:* I'm excited about the news.
- **excellent at / in** — xuất sắc. *Ex:* My teacher is excellent at English.
- **eager for** — hào hứng. *Ex:* I am eager for my next trip.
- **eligible for** — đủ điều kiện. *Ex:* Only those over 70 are eligible for the payment.
- **enthusiastic about** — hứng thú. *Ex:* You don't sound enthusiastic about the idea.
- **experienced in** — có kinh nghiệm. *Ex:* He's experienced in looking after animals.
- **envious of** (= jealous) — ghen tị. *Ex:* They were envious of his success.

### F
- **famous for** — nổi tiếng. *Ex:* Hanoi is famous for "pho".
- **fed up with / about** — chán ngấy. *Ex:* I got fed up with his constant complaining.
- **fond of** — ưa thích. *Ex:* I'm fond of cooking.
- **frightened of** — sợ. *Ex:* I've always been frightened of snakes.
- **furious at** — giận dữ. *Ex:* They were furious at not being invited.
- **faithful to** (= loyal) — trung thành. *Ex:* He remained faithful to the ideals of the party.
- **familiar with** — quen thuộc. *Ex:* He is familiar with me.
- **friendly with** — thân thiện. *Ex:* I am friendly with the people.
- **free of / from** — không chứa/không bị ảnh hưởng bởi. *Ex:* free from difficulty/doubt/fear.

### G
- **glad about** — vui mừng. *Ex:* He was glad about that.
- **guilty of / about** — cảm thấy có lỗi. *Ex:* I felt guilty about not visiting my parents more often.
- **generous with** — rộng rãi. *Ex:* To be generous with your time.
- **gentle with** — nhẹ nhàng, lịch thiệp. *Ex:* He is gentle with women and children.
- **good at** — giỏi. *Ex:* I'm good at English.
- **grateful to** — biết ơn. *Ex:* I am grateful to all the teachers for their help.

### H
- **happy about / with sb/sth** — hạnh phúc, hài lòng. *Ex:* I'm not happy with his work this term.
- **hopeless at** — vô vọng. *Ex:* I'm hopeless at science.
- **hopeful of / about** — hi vọng. *Ex:* He is not hopeful about the outcome of the interview.

### I
- **involved in / with** — liên quan đến. *Ex:* the costs involved in the project.
- **interested in** — quan tâm, thích thú. *Ex:* I'm interested in football.
- **incapable of** — không có khả năng. *Ex:* incapable of any original thought.
- **identical with / to** — giống. *Ex:* This house is identical to the one where I lived as a child.
- **immune to / from** — miễn dịch. *Ex:* Adults are often immune to German measles.
- **impressed with / by** — ấn tượng. *Ex:* We were impressed by her enthusiasm.
- **inferior to** — kém hơn. *Ex:* Modern music is often considered inferior to that of the past.
- **indifferent to** — thờ ơ. *Ex:* cannot afford to be indifferent to public opinion.
- **innocent of** — vô tội. *Ex:* She was found innocent of any crime.

### J
- **jealous of** — ghen ghét, đố kỵ. *Ex:* Other girls were jealous of her good looks.

### K
- **keen on** — thích thú. *Ex:* I'm keen on playing soccer.
- **kind to** — tốt bụng. *Ex:* He was very kind to me.

### L
- **late for** — muộn. *Ex:* He was ten minutes late for school.
- **limited to** — hạn chế. *Ex:* Racial problems are not limited to the south.
- **lucky at** — may mắn. *Ex:* I'm lucky at winning this trip.

### N
- **nervous of / about** — lo lắng. *Ex:* She was nervous about walking home so late.
- **notorious for** — nổi tiếng vì tiếng xấu. *Ex:* notorious for its appalling prison conditions.

### O
- **opposed to** — phản đối, bất đồng. *Ex:* He was bitterly opposed to the war.

### P
- **proud of** — tự hào về. *Ex:* They are proud of their daughter.
- **pleased with** — hài lòng với. *Ex:* I'm very pleased with the results.
- **patient with** — nhẫn nại. *Ex:* Susan's very patient with the children.
- **pessimistic about** — bi quan. *Ex:* Doctors are pessimistic about his chances.
- **polite to** — lịch sự. *Ex:* You must be more polite to the customers.
- **popular with** — được yêu thích bởi số đông. *Ex:* popular with middle-class voters.
- **punished for** — bị trừng phạt. *Ex:* He was punished for being late.

### Q
- **qualified for** — đủ điều kiện. *Ex:* He is qualified for this dangerous mission.

### R
- **responsible for** — chịu trách nhiệm về. *Ex:* You must be responsible for paying these bills.
- **ready for** — sẵn sàng. *Ex:* I'm ready for the exam.
- **related to** — liên quan đến. *Ex:* He was related to that crime.
- **relevant to** — liên quan đến. *Ex:* the facts relevant to the case.
- **respectful for / of** — tôn trọng. *Ex:* I'm respectful of your opinion.
- **be / get rid of** — tống khứ. *Ex:* We got rid of all the old furniture.

### S
- **sick of** — chán ngấy. *Ex:* I'm sick of waiting around like this.
- **sorry about / for** — hối hận. *Ex:* Are you sorry for what you've done?
- **scared of** — sợ hãi. *Ex:* All small children are scared of ghosts.
- **similar to** — tương tự. *Ex:* Your hat is similar to mine.
- **surprised at / by** — ngạc nhiên. *Ex:* We were surprised at your behavior.
- **shocked at / by** — sốc. *Ex:* I was shocked at the news of her death.
- **satisfied with** — hài lòng với. *Ex:* He felt satisfied with his job.
- **sensitive to / about** — nhạy cảm. *Ex:* She is very sensitive to other people's feelings.
- **successful in** — thành công. *Ex:* successful in putting out the fire.
- **serious about** — nghiêm túc. *Ex:* I'm serious about my work.
- **skillful at** — khéo léo. *Ex:* He is skillful at inventing excuses.
- **slow at** — chậm. *Ex:* She is slow at making up her mind.
- **suitable for / to** — thích hợp. *Ex:* This programme is not suitable for children.
- **sure of / about** — chắc chắn. *Ex:* I'm not sure of the results.
- **suspicious of / about** — nghi ngờ. *Ex:* I'm always suspicious about his kindness.
- **sympathetic to / towards** — thông cảm. *Ex:* I did not feel sympathetic towards Kate.

### T
- **tired of** — chán vì cái gì. *Ex:* I am tired of studying all the time.
- **tired from** — mệt vì làm gì. *Ex:* I am tired from teaching all day.
- **terrified of** — khiếp sợ. *Ex:* I'm terrified of spiders.
- **thankful to do sth / for sth** — biết ơn. *Ex:* I was thankful to see they'd all arrived safely.
- **troubled with** — lo lắng. *Ex:* Don't be troubled with that.
- **typical of** — điển hình. *Ex:* This meal is typical of local cookery.

### U
- **used to** — quen với. *Ex:* I'm used to getting up early.
- **upset about** — buồn về. *Ex:* I was upset about losing my money.
- **unaware of** — không nhận thức được. *Ex:* He was unaware of my presence.

### W
- **worried about** — lo lắng. *Ex:* He is always worried about his wife.
- **wrong with / about** — sai về. *Ex:* There's something wrong with the printer.

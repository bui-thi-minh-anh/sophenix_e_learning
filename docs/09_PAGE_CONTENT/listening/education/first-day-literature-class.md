# First Day of the Literature Class

- **Level:** A2 · **Topic:** Education · **Audio:** Classroom conversation
- **Speakers:** Mr. Harrison (male, British) · Student (female, American)

## Transcript

Teacher: Good morning, everyone, and welcome to your first English literature class. My name's Mr. Harrison.

Teacher: This term, we'll read three classic novels, starting with Great Expectations. Um, does everyone have the syllabus?

Student: Sorry, Mr. Harrison. I couldn't find it. Where can I get a copy?

Teacher: No worries. It's on the university portal, under Course Documents. You can print it there.

Student: Oh, great. And do we need to buy the books ourselves?

Teacher: Good question. The library has copies, but I'd recommend buying your own so you can take notes.

Student: Right. How much of the reading should we do before next week?

Teacher: Just the first three chapters. We'll discuss them together on Monday.

Student: Perfect. Thank you so much.

Teacher: You're welcome. Let's have a wonderful term.

## Grammar Focus

**will / 'll — kế hoạch tương lai.** Dùng khi nói về việc sắp làm: *we'll read*,
*We'll discuss them on Monday*. Dạng rút gọn: *we will → we'll*.

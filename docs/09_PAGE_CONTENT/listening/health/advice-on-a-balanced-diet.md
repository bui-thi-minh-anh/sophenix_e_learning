# Advice on a Balanced Diet

- **Level:** B1 · **Topic:** Health · **Audio:** Hospital consultation
- **Speakers:** Doctor (female, British) · Patient (male, American)

## Transcript

Doctor: So, how have you been feeling since your last visit?

Patient: Honestly, a bit tired. I don't have much energy in the afternoons.

Doctor: I see. Well, a balanced diet can make a real difference to your energy levels.

Patient: I thought a diet was just about losing weight.

Doctor: Not at all. It's about giving your body the nutrients it needs. Try to fill half your plate with vegetables and fruit.

Patient: Okay. What about protein?

Doctor: Lean proteins like chicken, fish, or lentils are great for building strength. And try to cut down on processed foods and sugar.

Patient: That's the hard part. I mean, I love a sugary snack.

Doctor: Small, consistent changes work best. Also, drink plenty of water and only eat when you're genuinely hungry.

Patient: That's really helpful. Thank you, doctor.

## Grammar Focus

**Lời khuyên với 'try to + V' và 'should'.** Bác sĩ dùng để gợi ý nhẹ nhàng:
*Try to fill…*, *try to cut down…*. Có thể thay bằng *You should…* trang trọng hơn.

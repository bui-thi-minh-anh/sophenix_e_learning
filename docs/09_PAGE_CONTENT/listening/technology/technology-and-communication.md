# How Technology Changed Communication

- **Level:** C1 · **Topic:** Technology · **Audio:** Interview
- **Speakers:** Radio host (female, American) · Dr. Alan Reed (male, British)

## Transcript

Host: Welcome back to Tech Talk. Today we're asking how technology has changed the way we communicate. I'm joined by Dr. Alan Reed.

Expert: Thanks for having me. It's a fascinating topic.

Host: So, how different is communication today compared to, say, twenty years ago?

Expert: Well, it's dramatically different. Twenty years ago, we relied on letters or expensive phone calls. Now we can video call anyone, anywhere, for free.

Host: That sounds entirely positive. Are there any downsides?

Expert: Absolutely. Constant connectivity affects our mental health. Many people struggle to disconnect from work emails or social media.

Host: I mean, I check my phone the moment I wake up. Is that a problem?

Expert: For a lot of people, yes. It's essential to set healthy boundaries with our devices.

Host: So what's your advice for our listeners?

Expert: Remember that technology should be a tool that enhances our lives, not something that controls them.

## Grammar Focus

**Present perfect — 'has changed'.** Diễn tả thay đổi bắt đầu trong quá khứ và còn
ảnh hưởng tới hiện tại: *technology **has changed** the way we communicate*.
Công thức: has/have + quá khứ phân từ.

# A Walking Tour of Edinburgh

- **Level:** B1 · **Topic:** Tourism · **Audio:** Tour guide speech
- **Speakers:** Tour guide (male, British) · Tourist (female, Australian)

## Transcript

Guide: Good morning, everyone, and welcome to our walking tour of historic Edinburgh. I'll be your guide today.

Guide: We'll start right here at Edinburgh Castle, which has stood on this volcanic rock for centuries.

Tourist: Excuse me, how long does the whole tour take?

Guide: About two hours, with a short break halfway. We'll walk down the Royal Mile at a gentle pace.

Tourist: Lovely. Will we have time to take photos?

Guide: Of course. There'll be plenty of photo opportunities, especially near the hidden alleyways.

Tourist: Great. And is it safe to carry my bag around?

Guide: It's generally very safe, but do keep an eye on your belongings in the crowded areas.

Tourist: Thanks. One more thing, where does the tour finish?

Guide: We'll end at the Palace of Holyroodhouse, right at the bottom of the Royal Mile.

## Grammar Focus

**will / 'll — lịch trình & dự định.** Hướng dẫn viên dùng để báo trước các chặng:
*We'll start…*, *We'll walk…*, *We'll end…*. Dạng rút gọn: *we will → we'll*.

# Booking a Room by Phone

- **Level:** B1 · **Topic:** Travel · **Duration:** ~41s
- **Audio Type:** Phone conversation
- **Speakers:** Hotel receptionist (female) · Guest (male) · **Accent:** American / British

---

## Transcript

R: Good afternoon, Riverside Hotel, this is Megan speaking. How can I help you?

G: Oh, hi. Um... I'd like to make a reservation for two nights, please.

R: Of course. And what dates were you looking at?

G: The fourteenth and fifteenth of March. A double room, if you've got one.

R: Let me just check our availability... Right, yes, we do have a double free for those nights.

G: Great. And, er... what's the rate?

R: It's ninety dollars a night, and breakfast is complimentary.

G: Perfect. What time's check-in?

R: From two p.m. We just take a small deposit to confirm the booking. I'll email you the details.

G: Lovely, thanks.

R: You're all set. Your reference number is B-4021.

## Grammar Focus

**would like to + động từ nguyên thể** — cách nói lịch sự để bày tỏ mong muốn,
thường dùng khi đặt chỗ, gọi món, yêu cầu dịch vụ. *I would* → *I'd*.

> *I'd like to make a reservation.*
